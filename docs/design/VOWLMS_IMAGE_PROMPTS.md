# VowLMS Image Direction and Prompts

These prompts preserve the established image direction for future approved generation. Do not generate text, logos, certificates, partner marks, or accreditation symbols inside images.

## Shared Direction

Premium documentary-style education photography; authentic South African setting; diverse learners and professionals; natural expressions; practical activity; clean contemporary environments; navy, cyan, white, and restrained warm accents; balanced daylight; credible rather than staged; no visible brand names; no generated text; generous composition space for responsive crops.

## Hero Ecosystem

Wide cinematic scene of South African adult learners and young professionals using laptops and tablets in a modern hybrid learning space, with a facilitator supporting collaboration and subtle Skills Practice equipment visible in the background. Confident, focused expressions; premium natural lighting; deep navy and cyan accents; realistic editorial photography; usable dark negative space on the left for a website headline; 16:9 desktop composition with a safe central mobile crop.

## Academy Network

Connected learning environment showing several authentic education activities in one coherent scene: digital upskilling, practical trade instruction, culinary learning, school support, sport coaching, business collaboration, and online study. Human-first documentary photography, South African context, consistent lighting and grading, no collage borders, no text or institutional logos, wide 16:9 framing.

## Dashboard Experience

Over-the-shoulder view of an adult learner using a clean learning dashboard on a laptop, with a tablet nearby and a facilitator in soft focus. The real focus is learner confidence and progress, not readable interface text. Contemporary workspace, premium but attainable, natural daylight, balanced navy/cyan accents, 3:2 composition with room for responsive cropping.

## Skills Practice Lab

South African learner completing a supervised immersive or simulation-based practical exercise in a credible training lab. Facilitator observes safely; equipment is realistic and non-branded; visual emphasis on applied skill, confidence, and feedback. Clean contemporary space, subtle cyan technical lighting balanced with natural skin tones, wide 16:9 framing, no science-fiction spectacle and no generated text.
